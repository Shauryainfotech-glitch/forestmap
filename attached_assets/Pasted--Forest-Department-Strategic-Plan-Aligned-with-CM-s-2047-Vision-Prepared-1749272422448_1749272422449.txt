## **1. THREE-TIER VISION FRAMEWORK**

### **üéØ SHORT-TERM VISION (2025-2029)**
**"Digital Forest Maharashtra - Climate Resilient & Citizen-Centric"**

**Annual Targets for 2025-26:**
- Achieve 35% forest cover (currently ~21%)
- Plant 50 crore saplings with 80% survival rate
- Digitize 100% forest land records
- Implement AI-based forest monitoring
- Zero human-wildlife conflict incidents through smart solutions

### **üåø MEDIUM-TERM VISION (2029-2035)**
**"Maharashtra @75 - Carbon Neutral Forest Ecosystem"**

- Achieve 40% forest cover
- Establish Maharashtra as India's leading carbon credit state
- Complete ecosystem restoration of degraded lands
- Create 50,000 green jobs in forest-based economy

### **üå≥ LONG-TERM VISION (2035-2047)**
**"Bharat @100 - Global Leader in Sustainable Forestry"**

- Achieve 45% forest cover (exceeding national target)
- Net-zero carbon emissions from forest sector
- Pioneer in climate adaptation and biodiversity conservation
- Model state for forest-based sustainable development
